Etrain was founded in 2016 to bridge the knowledge gap between colleges and industry. Founded by Ankush Singla, Kannu Mittal and Dhawal Parate,Etrain boasts of world-class teaching faculty and a state-of-art learning platform for Coding education with faculty alumni of IIT, Stanford, IIIT and Facebook.

Etrain teaches 10+ Programming courses in Foundation,l Advanced, Data & Development courses such as Machine Learning, Data Science, Web Development, Android and more.

Today, Our ecosystem comprises of 40,000+ students and alumni, 1000+ Campus Ambassadors, 2000+ Teaching Assistants, and 150+ employees.
